Event Fiesta

Discover the perfect event organizer effortlessly with Event Fiesta! This web app employs a sophisticated algorithm, considering your budget, guest count, and venue preferences. Applying SOLID principles like the Singleton pattern for efficiency and Composite pattern for comprehensive evaluation, Event Fiesta narrows down the top 5 organizers, each assigned a user-friendly score out of 5.
