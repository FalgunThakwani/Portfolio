Memories

Memories a beautifully designed mobile app for seamless photo sharing, organizing, and summarizing. Developed with Gestalt's Design Law in mind, this Figma-crafted app ensures a modern and intuitive UI. Designed UI for features like individual photo sharing in groups, camera-based searches, and easy video editing. Relive your memories effortlessly with Memories.

  • Transformed low-fidelity design into a high-fidelity prototype using Figma.

  • Understanding and designing for user needs and experience.

  • Sketched low-fidelity prototype based on a user-centered approach.
